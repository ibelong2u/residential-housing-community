Community Overview

Tierra del Rey is a new, 142-lot manufactured housing community located in Houston.

The community features two private, gated entrances, street lighting and generous common areas. The common areas include a community office, children's playground, picnic area and visitor parking. Additionally, each lot will have THREE dedicated parking spaces.

The community is partnering with the premiere home builders of Texas – Clayton Homes, Champions Homes, Oak Creek Homes, and Jessup Housing. Floor plans will range from 1,050 square feet to 1,215 square feet and two bedrooms, two bathrooms to three bedrooms, two bathrooms. Additionally, each home will include premium skirting, a private deck, and a private yard.

The community is centrally located, approximately 10 miles from downtown Houston. It enjoys easy access to major thoroughfares, including I-45, I-69, I-610 and Beltway 8.

With starting prices of $55,000, you won't want to miss the opportunity to live in this new community.
